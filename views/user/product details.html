Description

This sophisticated perfume combines rich notes of leather, wood & spices against a heart of lily, rose and soft amber creating a luxurious, yet accessible fragrance. Suitable for wear to the office or to that casual dinner date, this perfume is truly versatile and unmistakably masculine.

The opening bursts with fresh citrus and aromatic herbs, leading into a heart of spicy roses and earthy leather for an energetic, yet warm, well-rounded scent that shines with fragrance-wood heart notes, a blend of sandalwood, cedar, and patchouli. Perfect for those who value exceptional quality and unique scents.
